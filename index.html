Welcome to Troop 904!

Troop 904 was founded in 2014 with one central mission in mind: creating memories that will last a lifetime, and helping the environment along with it. We bring together participants from all walks of life for meaningful experiences year after year. Our immersive camping trips, hikes, and outings provide unique opportunities for personal growth and development, but always leave room for plenty of fun. Our Boy Scouts Troop incites a zest for learning and exploration, and participants leave feeling fulfilled and inspired. Ready to take part in the action? Come join us for your next adventure.
